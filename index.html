About Innovedex

Innovedex is a technology innovation initiative focused on robotics, artificial intelligence and automation education. The platform is designed to develop engineering mindset, real-world problem solving, and future-ready skills for youth.

Innovedex integrates hands-on learning with emerging technologies to prepare learners for smart industries and the digital economy.
